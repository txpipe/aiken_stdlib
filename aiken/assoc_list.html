aiken/assoc_list

A module for working with associative lists.

Associative lists are fundamentally ordered lists of key-value pairs, which preserve some invariants. In particular, each key is only present once in the list.

Note however that they arenâ€™t proper maps or dictionnaries! So, two lists constructed in a different order may not be equal (in the sense of the equality operator ==) even though they have the exact same elements.
